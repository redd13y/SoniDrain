About Us

iAcademy · Guadalupe Sector · Flood Intelligence System

About The Team

SoniDrain is a real-time flood monitoring system engineered by the first-year Software Engineering students of iAcademy. We deploy ultrasonic sensor nodes across drainage networks to deliver live water-level data — turning raw readings into actionable early warnings for the communities of Cebu.

// 01 · Purpose

Mission & Vision

01

// Mission

Smarter Flood
Intelligence

Our mission is to make flood data accessible, accurate, and actionable for every resident in flood-prone barangays. By combining low-cost sensor hardware with cloud-connected dashboards, SoniDrain bridges the gap between raw sensor readings and life-saving early warnings.

02

// Vision

A Safer,
Data-Driven City

We envision a future where local governments, barangay officials, and citizens have instant access to hydrological data — enabling proactive evacuation decisions rather than reactive responses. SoniDrain is our first step toward a city-wide environmental sensing network.

Built With

SoniDrain runs on a full stack built for reliability and real-time performance — from embedded microcontrollers at the sensor node, to a Supabase cloud backend, to a pure-JS frontend that renders live data without any heavyweight framework.
